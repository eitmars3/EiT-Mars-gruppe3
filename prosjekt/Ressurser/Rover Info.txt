Spectroscopy + Labs
-CHEMCAM Spectroscopy (chemistry imaging: two pieces of gear, one gives elemental compositions of rock and soil, the other provides pictures of sampling area). Composed of LIBS (analysis) and RIM (pics)
	-http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11214-012-9912-2 
	-http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0584854711000632
	-http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0584854712000043 (LIBS)
-REMS Environment  monitoring (humidity, pressure, temperature, wind speed etc)
-APXS: X-ray spectrometer: irridates samples with alpha particles, maps spectra of xrays for determining elemental composition of samples/relative abundances of materials 
-“CheMin” chemistry and minerology spectrometer: pulverizes samples and blasts them with xrays to determine crystal structures and therefore the material being examined
	-http://www.sciencemag.org/content/341/6153/1238932.short (Minerology)
-Sample Analysis at Mars (SAM) laboratory, performs precision measurements of gases in the atmosphere:
	-QMS mass spectrometer (determines mass?)
	-Gas chromatograph (separating and analyzing compositions of compounds)
	-Laser spectrometer 
		-http://www.sciencemag.org/content/341/6143/263.short 
		-http://www.sciencemag.org/content/341/6153/1238937.short 
		-http://www.lpi.usra.edu/meetings/lpsc2013/eposter/1366.pdf 
-RAD radiation assessment detector 
	-http://www.sciencemag.org/content/343/6169/1244797.short 
-DAN Dynamic Albedo of Neutrons: Measures hydrogen/water
